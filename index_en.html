About Kirkeibyen

We are a church that focuses on God and people. Our goal is to give everyone the opportunity to experience faith, follow Jesus as an example, and make a difference in the community we are part of.

We love community and want our meetings and activities to be relevant for everyday life. Here, there is room for everyone – children, young people and adults – and we hope that everyone who visits us feels welcome.

No matter if you come to Kolding, Fredericia or Haderslev, you will experience a church focused on faith, life and community.
